About Classics Inc.

Welcome to Classics Inc.

Timeless Comfort in the Heart of the City

Classics Inc. is a boutique hotel located in the heart of the city, blending timeless elegance with modern comfort. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our personalized service and thoughtfully designed spaces will make you feel right at home.

Just steps away from top attractions and cultural sites, our hotel offers a peaceful escape with easy access to everything you need.

What We Offer

Comfortable Rooms

Stylish and spacious rooms with premium bedding, smart TVs, and blackout curtains for a great night's sleep.

Free Breakfast

Start your day with our complimentary breakfast buffet featuring local and international options.

Fast Wi-Fi

Stay connected with free high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, ideal for both work and fun.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room? ▼
You can book a room online via our Rooms page or by contacting us directly.
Can I cancel my booking? ▼
Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before check-in for a full refund. You just need to write to us at info@classics.com with a receipt of payment attached, after which we can remove your reservation.
Is breakfast included? ▼
Yes, all room rates include complimentary breakfast.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards.
Are pets allowed in the hotel? ▼
Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in our hotel, except for service animals.
Is parking available? ▼
Yes, we offer free parking for all our guests.
